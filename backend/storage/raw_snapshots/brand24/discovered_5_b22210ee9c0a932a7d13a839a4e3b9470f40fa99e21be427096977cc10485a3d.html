Frequently
Asked Questions:

What are AI Insights?

AI Insights are weekly data-driven reports powered by artificial intelligence, designed to give organizations real-time visibility into brand health. These insights analyze metrics like reach, sentiment, and mentions. We designed them to help businesses quickly understand changes and receive recommendations for optimizing operations and strategies.

What is generative AI?

Generative AI is artificial intelligence that uses advanced algorithms to create content or predictions based on data analysis. In Brand24, it generates our insights and, in general, turns data into text.

What industries benefit from using AI Insights?

AI Insights benefit various sectors, from finance and healthcare to technology. They help companies from every industry across 154 countries monitor reputation, detect emerging trends, and manage crisis effectively.

How will AI Insights help my business?

AI Insights automates data analysis, providing a complete view of brand online activity. They enable business leaders to focus on planning the right strategy rather than routine data-gathering tasks.

How does AI Insights improve customer experience?

By tracking and analyzing sentiment, AI Insights reveals what drives your audience's positive or negative reactions. This lets businesses tailor strategies to customer needs, enhancing service quality and engagement.

What trends can AI Insights detect?

AI Insights can identify shifts in brand mentions, sentiment and highlight emerging topics. By predicting these changes, every organization can explore new opportunities and gain a competitive edge.

Can AI Insights spot emerging challenges?

Yes, AI Insights can identify potential threats by analyzing sentiment and reach trends. This early detection allows for proactive measures to manage reputational risks.

Can AI Insights support business growth?

Absolutely. The insights provide recommendations for optimization, from marketing to customer outreach, enabling businesses to implement strategies that promote efficiency and growth.

How does AI Insights save time on data analysis?

AI automates the aggregation and interpretation of data. Additionally, it surfaces the most important mentions from the previous week. This significantly reduces the manual effort required, allowing your company to invest this time into day-to-day operations and any innovation.
