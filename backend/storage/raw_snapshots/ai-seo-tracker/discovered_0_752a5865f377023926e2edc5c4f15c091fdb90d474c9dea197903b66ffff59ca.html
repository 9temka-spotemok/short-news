AI Search Market 2025: Monthly Active Users for ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity

Last updated: Sep 1, 2025

The AI search revolution is happening right now. By 2025, over 1 billion people use AI assistants every month. This is the fastest tech adoption we've ever seen.

Here are the numbers that matter for your business.

ChatGPT: The Clear Winner

OpenAI's ChatGPT is the biggest AI platform in the world. It now has around 1 billion monthly users globally.

The growth is massive:

  • 700 million weekly users in August 2025
  • 67.7 million monthly users just in the US
  • 2.5 billion daily prompts as of July 2025
  • 4x growth year-over-year

What this means: ChatGPT isn't just a tool anymore. It's where a billion people go to search for answers.

Claude: Growing Fast Among Professionals

Anthropic's Claude focuses on safety and smart responses. It hit 30 million monthly users in Q2 2025.

Claude's key numbers:

  • 25 billion API calls per month
  • 2-3% of the AI chatbot market
  • Popular with startups and developers
  • Strong growth from 16-18.9 million users earlier in 2025

What this means: Claude is becoming the go-to AI for technical work and professional tasks.

Perplexity: The Search Specialist

Perplexity AI is different. It's an AI-powered search engine that gives you sources with every answer.

Perplexity's growth story:

  • 22 million monthly users in mid-2025
  • Up from 15 million earlier this year
  • 780 million monthly searches
  • 120 million website visitors per month
  • 2 million daily users

What this means: Perplexity is winning with people who want accurate, sourced answers.
