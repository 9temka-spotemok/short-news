Performance Index

See how your website truly performs in SERP

The goal of rank tracking is to know the organic traffic potential of your website. That's why we've developed the Performance Index based on the current keyword positions and their search volumes.

Daily rank updates & historical data

Get rank updates on a daily basis

See the rankings of your website day-by-day and check them in the SERP previews. Besides that, we will give you heads up about all the important rank changes via email alerts.

Location and device-based results

Track rankings in any location, on any device

Having precise local data is crucial for rank tracking. We have more than 65k locations including states, counties, cities or DMA regions. Both desktop & mobile search results are supported.
