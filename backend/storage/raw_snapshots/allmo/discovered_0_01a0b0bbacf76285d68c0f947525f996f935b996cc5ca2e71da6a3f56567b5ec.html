Methodology

Approach

The AI Trend Report is based on a diverse set of prompts, including AI-generated autocomplete suggestions.

Topics are selected from major news outlet categories and focus on broadly relevant or ongoing issues, allowing for consistent weekly tracking using similar prompts.

While some bias may occur (e.g. toward localized or niche topics), the report will evolve to include more themes, question types, and language models over time.
