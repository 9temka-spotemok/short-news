LLM Tracking

Track and analyze your LLM performance with AccuLLM

AccuLLM gives you insights into how your brand performs in ChatGPT, Perplexity, AI Overview, and AI Mode. Track your visibility across prompts, monitor sentiment, learn which sources are cited, and benchmark your performance against competitors.

1

Average rank

Understand your average rank across the LLMs based on the prompts you track.

2

Total brand mentions

Learn how many times the LLMs mention your brand in their prompt responses.

3

Domain citations

Know how often the LLMs cite your domain and identify your top-performing pages.

Track your visibility

ChatGPT, Perplexity, AI Overview, and AI Mode

AccuLLM lets you track your performance in ChatGPT, Perplexity, AI Overview, and AI Mode. Learn how visible you are in LLMs based on how often your brand or domain is mentioned across your tracked prompts.

Analyze the sentiment

AccuLLM’s sentiment analysis shows whether the LLMs talk positively, neutrally, or negatively about your brand. We give you a score from 1 to 100, letting you know how the LLMs present your brand to users.

Identify cited sources

Track which domains and landing pages the LLMs cite most often. This way, you can identify influential sources where you should build your presence.

Get prompt suggestions

We suggest what prompts to track based on your brand and different parts of your business. Thus, you ensure that you track the most relevant conversations.

Monitor competitors

Benchmark your performance

Learn who your competitors are and compare your brand to competitors across average visibility, rank, and sentiment. AccuLLM gives you insights into how your competitors perform in LLMs, allowing you to benchmark performance.
