What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing your content so that it gets cited in AI-generated answers from platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. Unlike traditional SEO that focuses on ranking in search results, GEO ensures AI systems actually pull your brand, links, and insights into their responses.

The fundamental difference? SEO gets you seen on Google, GEO gets you mentioned by AI. When someone asks ChatGPT for recommendations, you want your product to be in that answer â€“ not just ranking on page one of Google where fewer people are clicking through.
