Top 30 interesting stats about Indexing and SEO in 2025

SEO is a mixture of known ranking factors, guesstimated ranking factors, and testing (over and over and over). That's why SEO statistics are so important.

Deepti

Mar 6, 2024 — 3 min read
  1. Indexing is the process of adding web pages to a search engine's database. This helps the search engine understand the content of a website and how it should be returned in search results.
  2. Indexing helps search engines discover and crawl new content on a website, leading to faster inclusion in search results.
  3. Google's algorithm is designed to prioritise the indexing of high-quality, frequently updated pages. Googlebot crawls over 25 billion pages per day, but prioritises freshness, authority, and sitemap signals. (Google, 2025)
  4. 80% of e-commerce pages not indexed within 48 hours never reach the top 100 search results. (Ahrefs, 2025)
  5. Index bloat affects 35% of enterprise websites, causing crawl budget waste and diluted rankings. (Screaming Frog, 2025)
  6. Over 70% of search results feature AI-generated summaries (AI Overviews), transforming content discovery. (Google I/O, 2025)
  7. Google’s crawl frequency for frequently updated sites increased by 24% due to real-time API integration and structured data improvements. (Search Engine Journal, 2025)
  8. Sites with schema markup see 17% higher crawl frequency and faster indexing. (Schema.org Trends Report, 2025)
  9. Topical authority is now a top 3 ranking factor, especially for content clusters. (Semrush State of SEO, 2025)
  10. Improved internal linking leads to 38% faster indexing and deeper crawl depth. (Ahrefs, 2025)
  11. Pages optimized for Core Web Vitals index 22% faster and rank higher in mobile-first search. (Google Search Central, 2025)
  12. Only 54% of submitted sitemaps are fully indexed due to issues like thin content and duplication. (Indexing Lab, 2025)
  13. E-commerce sites using auto-indexing plugins see 40% more pages indexed during sales spikes. (Shopify SEO Benchmark, 2025)
  14. News articles lose 65% of traffic potential if not indexed within 30 minutes of publishing. (Google News SEO Guide, 2025)
  15. 31% of SEO agencies use indexing automation tools like Indexly or Indexmenow. (Moz Survey, 2025)
  16. Auto-deindexing tools reduce index bloat by up to 47%. (Indexly, 2025)
  17. Bulk indexing APIs cut manual submission time by 90%, aiding enterprise SEO teams. (BrightEdge, 2025)
  18. 92% of searches occur on mobile devices, emphasizing mobile-first indexing and fast load times. (Statista, 2025)
  19. Voice searches prioritize indexed FAQs and featured snippets, but 61% of sites don’t optimize for these. (Search Engine Land, 2025)
  20. Sites monitoring indexing status daily with alerts see 28% fewer missed opportunities in seasonal/time-sensitive content. (Indexly, 2025)
  21. Google processes over 8.5 billion searches daily, about 99,000 per second. (SEO PowerSuite, 2025)
  22. 94% of user clicks go to organic listings rather than paid ads. (SEO PowerSuite, 2025)
  23. Featured snippets have the highest click-through rate at 42.9%. (AIOSEO, 2025)
  24. 75% of users never scroll past the first page of search results. (AIOSEO, 2025)
  25. Content over 3,000 words generates 3x more traffic, 4x more shares, and 3.5x more backlinks than average-length posts. (AIOSEO, 2025)
  26. Google uses over 200 ranking factors, with content quality, backlinks, and search intent as top influencers. (AIOSEO, 2025)
  27. The #1 organic search result receives 39.8% of all clicks, and moving from #2 to #1 boosts clicks by 74.5%. (AIOSEO, 2025)
  28. Rich results attract 58% of clicks compared to 41% for standard results. (AIOSEO, 2025)
  29. Only 0.78% of users click results on Google’s second page. (AIOSEO, 2025)
  30. SEO leads close at 14.6%, compared to 1.7% for outbound leads, making SEO 8.5x more effective for conversions. (Search Atlas, 2025)
